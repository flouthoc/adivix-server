- hack module to support postgresql
  - pgsql.h + pgsql.c
  - tables
    - users
      - id, name, passhash, priv, avatar
    - privmsgs
      - sender, recipients, perm, attachments, size, body
    - files
      - uid, gid, perm, size, data
    - newsgroups
      - id, name, perm, flags (priv, moder, ...),
      	members (none -> public, moders)
    - news
      - uid, ngid, perm
    - posts
      - uid, fid (forum), date, perm
